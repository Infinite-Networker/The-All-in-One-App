Core Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Engineered for digital creators, marketers, and social media managers who refuse to compromise on efficiency or experience.

⚡

One-Tap Universal Engagement

Like, comment, and follow across all 6 platforms simultaneously. One button replaces six separate apps and dozens of taps.

🔗

Multi-Platform Integration

Manage multiple accounts and identities across Instagram, X, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, and YouTube in one unified interface.

🔄

Real-Time Content Sync

WebSocket-powered unified feed that aggregates posts instantly from all connected accounts — no manual refreshing required.

📊

Engagement Analytics

Visualise growth and interaction patterns with a cross-platform analytics dashboard — follower trends, engagement rates, best posting times.

⚙️

Custom Interaction Rules

Set per-platform engagement preferences, schedules, and filters. Automate what makes sense, stay hands-on where it matters.

🔐

OAuth 2.0 Security

Industry-standard OAuth 2.0 per platform. Zero raw credential storage. AES-256 encryption at rest, JWT tokens in transit.

🌙

Dark & Light Mode

A premium aesthetic that adapts to your preference. Every pixel designed for comfort during extended sessions.

🤖

AI Content Recommendations

Coming soon — AI-inferred optimal posting windows and content performance predictions based on your historical engagement data.

📅

Scheduled Posting Queue

Coming soon — plan and schedule posts across all platforms from one queue. Set it and let the engine handle delivery.

Integrations

All the platforms.
One command centre.
