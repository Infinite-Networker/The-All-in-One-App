## 🖼️ Logo Concepts

### Concept A: The Hub
- Six platform-coloured dots arranged in a circle
- Thin connecting lines between each dot
- Cherry red lightning bolt at centre
- Circular container with cherry gradient background

### Concept B: The Infinity Connect
- Stylised infinity symbol (∞) where each loop contains platform icons
- Cherry red gradient treatment
- Clean, minimal wordmark below

### Concept C: The All-in-One Monogram
- Bold "A1" letterform (All-in-One)
- Surrounded by six micro platform icons
- Dark background with cherry accent

### Usage Rules
- Always use the logo on dark or light backgrounds only (never on busy images)
- Minimum size: 32px × 32px for digital, 1cm × 1cm for print
- Never stretch, rotate, or recolour the logo
- Always maintain clear space equal to the logo's height on all sides

## 📲 App Icon Specification

### Sizes Required
- iOS: 1024×1024px (App Store), 180×180px (@3x), 120×120px (@2x), 60×60px (@1x)
- Android: 512×512px (Play Store), Adaptive icon 108×108dp

### Design
- Background: Cherry red gradient, slightly rounded square (iOS), or adaptive (Android)
- Foreground: White on the centre symbol
- No text in the app icon

## 🗣️ Voice & Tone

### How We Talk
We speak like a **confident, knowledgeable creator** — not a corporation. Our voice is:

- **Direct**: "One tap." Not "Easily engage with a single tap of your finger."
- **Personal**: "I built this because..." Not "This product was developed..."
- **Enthusiastic but not hyped**: We believe in what we make. We don't oversell it.
- **Technical when needed**: We respect our users' intelligence.

### Writing Examples

✅ **Good:**
> "I built this because I was tired of jumping between six different apps just to stay active online."

❌ **Avoid:**
> "Our innovative solution leverages cutting-edge technology to provide a seamless cross-platform engagement experience."

✅ **Good:**
> "One tap. Every platform. Zero friction."

❌ **Avoid:**
> "Effortlessly manage all of your social media accounts in one convenient location!"
